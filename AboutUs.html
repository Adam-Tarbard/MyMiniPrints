About Us - Reviews 

Hi, I am Sue Tarbard and am passionate about what I create. I have had a creative streak for as long as I can remember and it is wonderful to be able to utilise this within my work. As a child I had a love of drawing and colouring alongside painting numerous plaster of paris models that I made in the kitchen. As an outlet for my creative side whilst working in London for a large Investment Company, I enrolled on weekly calligraphy classes. Leaving the corporate world in 2004 to be with my young children gave me the opportunity to embark on a new career producing various unique keepsakes for little ones. 
At My Mini Prints I feel privileged to produce a range of keepsakes, that will be treasured by parents and children of today, and passed down through the generations of tomorrow.
